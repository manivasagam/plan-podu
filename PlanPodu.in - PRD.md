# **Product Requirement Document (PRD): PlanPodu.in**

## **1\. Product Vision**

To become the "Digital Swiss Army Knife" for property buyers, builders, and citizens in Tamil Nadu by simplifying complex legal calculations and government processes into 1-click tools.

## **2\. Target Audience**

* **Property Buyers:** People confused by Guideline Values and Stamp Duty.  
* **Plot Owners:** Users looking to verify if their land is DTCP/CMDA approved.  
* **Real Estate Agents:** Professionals needing quick area conversions for clients.  
* **SME Builders:** Small-scale contractors in towns like Villupuram needing quick Vastu or FSI checks.

---

## **3\. Core Features & Tool Specifications (MVP)**

### **Tool 1: The TN "On-Road" Property Cost Calculator**

* **Logic:** Calculates the total cost of buying a property beyond just the purchase price.  
* **Inputs:**  
  * Purchase Price vs. Guideline Value (uses the higher of the two).  
  * Buyer Category (Male/Female/Joint \- *to account for potential 2026 concessions*).  
  * Deed Type (Sale, Gift, Settlement, Partition).  
* **Output:** Breakdown of **7% Stamp Duty**, **4% Registration Fee**, and estimated document writer/legal fees.  
* **SEO Hook:** "Stamp duty calculator Tamil Nadu 2026", "Property registration charges in \[City Name\]".

### **Tool 2: The "Manai Adi" & Area Converter**

* **Logic:** Converts traditional TN units to modern sq. ft. and hectares.  
* **Inputs:** Number \+ Unit dropdown.  
* **Supported Units:** **Cent, Ground, Kuzhi, Acre, Hectare, Sq. Metre, Sq. Feet.**  
* **Output:** Instant conversion table across all other units.  
* **SEO Hook:** "Cent to Sqft converter", "1 Ground in Sqft Tamil Nadu".

### **Tool 3: CMDA vs DTCP Jurisdiction Finder**

* **Logic:** A simple "Search by Location" tool to tell users which authority governs their land.  
* **Data:** A mapped list of Chennai Metropolitan Area (CMA) villages vs. the rest of TN.  
* **Feature:** Provides a "Checklist for Approval" based on the result (e.g., if DTCP, show "LPA No." and "Panchayat NOC" requirements).  
* **SEO Hook:** "Is my land under DTCP or CMDA?", "DTCP approval process in Villupuram".

### **Tool 4: AI-Vastu Floor Plan Drafter**

* **Logic:** Uses Gemini API to suggest room placements based on Vastu.  
* **Inputs:** Plot Facing (East, West, North, South) \+ Plot Size.  
* **Output:** A text-based or simple SVG diagram showing where the Kitchen (Agni), Master Bedroom (Niruthi), and Pooja Room should be.  
* **SEO Hook:** "East facing 30x40 house plan Vastu", "Simple Vastu plan for North facing plot".

## **4\. Technical Requirements**

* **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router) for SEO-friendly static generation of tool pages.  
* **Performance:** All tools must have a **Lighthouse Score of 95+**.  
* **Mobile First:** 90% of TN users will access this via WhatsApp links on mobile.  
* **Language:** Toggle for **English and Tamil** (crucial for local trust).
